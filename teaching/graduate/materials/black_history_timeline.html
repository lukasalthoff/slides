Black History Timeline (mobile)
—
Drag to pan • Scroll to zoom • Drag edges to extend/shorten

This interactive visualization presents a curated overview of Black American history. Pan by dragging and zoom with your mouse or trackpad. Use the left/right handles to adjust the time span. Search to highlight matching events and eras. Click any item to read a concise description and open its Wikipedia page. This selection of events, eras, and people is far from exhaustive and will continue to evolve. Created by Lukas Althoff (lalthoff@stanford.edu) with the help of OpenAI GPT-5.
